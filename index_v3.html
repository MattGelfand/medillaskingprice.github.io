The Asking Price

Culture comes at a cost

What do our identities cost us? Surely we pay a certain fee – money, time, energy, other opportunities – for each of us to follow our hearts and lead the life we love. This site introduces six cultures, six economies, and six interactive articles, each by a team of graduate student journalists. In exploring different media and formats, the students discovered there are infinite ways to tell a story and all of them have landed on the same question.

What is the asking price?
